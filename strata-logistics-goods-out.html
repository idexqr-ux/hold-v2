Pallet Readiness

Check pallet labels, count, stability and customer allocation before moving to dispatch.

Wrapping Condition

Confirm wrapping is secure and transport-ready. Report damage or instability before carrier handover.

Shared route

Goods Out Actions

Start dispatch scan

Human Judgement

If the pick, pallet condition, carrier timing or route condition is wrong, pause the dispatch path before release.

Damage Reported

Outbound damage report opened. Keep the pallet out of carrier release until condition and customer impact are reviewed.

Mismatch Reported

Mismatch recorded between pick request, pallet label, customer order or dispatch allocation.

Supervisor Requested

Supervisor request raised with pick, pallet readiness, forklift and Bay C03 allocation context attached.

Hold Area B Move

Hold Area B movement prepared for the outbound exception. Keep customer order and dispatch reference attached.

Route Obstruction

Route obstruction reported. Pause movement to Bay C03 until route status and forklift coordination are confirmed.

Other Logistics Issue

Capture an outbound issue that affects pick confidence, route readiness or dispatch bay allocation.
