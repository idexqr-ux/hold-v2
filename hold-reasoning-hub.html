Reasoning overview

Routes into operational understanding

Hold brings together trusted context, operational memory and connected information so people can better understand what is happening before deciding what to do next. Bay C03 shows how Hold brings scattered operational information together so people can understand what changed, what needs attention and who owns the current review. Operational memory also preserves what the organisation learned, so future reviews do not start from zero. Hold also keeps responsibility visible by showing which role carries the current understanding forward before action is taken.
