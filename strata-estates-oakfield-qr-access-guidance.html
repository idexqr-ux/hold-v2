Delivery Routes

Use shared route

Use the shared access route only. Confirm low-loader route, turning space, banksman need and unloading point before heavy plant arrives.

Restricted Access

Keep clear

Do not block the utility compound entrance, neighbouring access, soft verge, turning area or active contractor exclusion zone.

Pedestrian Awareness

Shared movement

Watch for pedestrians, visitors, contractors and neighbouring users crossing shared routes during deliveries or temporary diversions.

Temporary Diversions

Active controls

Temporary diversions must keep visitor parking, neighbouring access, contractor delivery routes and emergency routes visible.

Contractor Movement

Coordinate arrivals

Contractors should report arrival, confirm permit status, avoid blocking shared access and wait for route release if plant is moving.

Emergency Access

Keep open

Emergency access must remain open through gates, turning areas and shared routes. Escalate any blockage immediately.

Environmental Awareness

Ground condition

Wet ground, rutting, mud transfer, dust and spill pathways can change whether the planned access route remains suitable.

Escalation Route

Human decision

Use escalation where access, contractor movement, pedestrian safety or emergency route availability needs Estates judgement.

Delivery Route Check

Delivery route check requested. Confirm route, turning area, unloading point, low-loader constraints and shared access impact.

Access Assistance

Access assistance requested. Confirm vehicle type, turning constraint, low-loader need, pedestrian exposure, escort need and emergency route impact.

Restricted Access

Restricted access issue recorded. Check utility compound, neighbouring access, soft verge, turning area and exclusion zone controls.

Pedestrian Concern

Pedestrian concern opened. Confirm who is exposed, where routes cross, what movement is planned and what temporary control is needed.

Diversion Change

Diversion change logged. Link the temporary route, affected users, signage, expected duration and emergency access status.

Contractor Movement

Contractor movement issue opened. Confirm arrival time, vehicle type, permit status, route release and any escort need.

Emergency Access

Emergency access support requested. Confirm gate, route, turning space, obstruction, contact point and responder access path.

Environmental Access Concern

Environmental access concern recorded. Check wet ground, rutting, mud transfer, dust, spill path and route suitability.

Estates Escalation

Estates escalation requested. Use where access safety, contractor movement, pedestrian exposure or emergency route availability needs a decision.

Other Access Issue

Other access issue opened. Capture the route, user, restriction, visibility concern and decision needed.
