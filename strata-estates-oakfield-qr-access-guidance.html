Access Instructions

  1. Use shared access route only
  2. Confirm low-loader route before heavy plant delivery
  3. Keep turning area clear during contractor attendance
  4. Avoid soft verge during wet conditions unless matting is in place
  5. Coordinate movements with neighbouring land access where required

Restriction Notes

Tight Manoeuvring Area

  • Utility compound entrance Large vehicle turning space limited during simultaneous site activity
  • Shared access route Neighbouring access must remain unobstructed at all times
  • Low-loader delivery route Advance review required before heavy equipment attendance
