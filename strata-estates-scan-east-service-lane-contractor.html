East Service Lane Works

Contractor guidance for drainage survey works near the east service lane.

Active Works

Access guidance

Neighbouring landowner access track operationally referred to as “The Willows”.

Use the north compound parking area while visitor parking is temporarily redirected. Follow site signage and avoid blocking shared access routes.

Before work starts

  • Confirm RAMS approval before entering the working area.
  • Check current drainage survey boundary with the estates contact.
  • Keep the east service lane clear for authorised access.

Operational note

Any change to parking, drainage access, or temporary restrictions should be reported back to the Estates dashboard.

Contractor Actions

Use these actions to report temporary restrictions, drainage access changes or operational issues back into Hold.

Report Access Issue

Report blocked access routes, vehicle conflicts or restricted entry points affecting operational movement.

  • Routes reported through Hold remain visible to Estates review teams.
  • Temporary restrictions may appear within operational dashboard notices.
Report Temporary Restriction

Record temporary parking restrictions, drainage barriers or shared route limitations affecting operational access.

Report Drainage Access Change

Record updated drainage survey boundaries or revised operational access requirements.

Confirm Works Complete

Confirm that drainage survey works have completed and temporary operational restrictions may be reviewed for removal.

Other Contractor Issue

Record operational concerns not covered by an existing contractor workflow category.
