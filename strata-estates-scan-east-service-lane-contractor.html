East Service Lane Works

Contractor guidance for drainage survey activity near the east service lane, shared access routes and inherited site risks.

Active works

RAMS Awareness

Before entry

Confirm the latest RAMS revision, permit controls, insurance status and supervisor release before entering the working area.

Authorised Works

Drainage survey

Activity is limited to the approved drainage survey boundary. Any change to scope, plant, timing or method needs Estates review.

Restricted Areas

Shared route

Keep the east service lane, visitor diversion, neighbouring access track and any signed exclusion zones clear during the works window.

Isolation Requirements

Check first

Do not disturb drains, covers, ducts or services until isolation need, utility notes and buried-service uncertainty have been checked.

Inherited Site Risks

Local knowledge

The access track is still known locally as "The Willows". Historic drainage changes and soft ground may not match the drawing.

Public & Shared Access

Keep open

Visitor parking is redirected to the north compound. Keep pedestrian movement, neighbouring access and emergency routes unobstructed.

Environmental Awareness

Runoff risk

Drainage access, standing water, silt movement, blocked covers and spill pathways should be reported before work continues.

Escalation Contacts

Estates route

Escalate permit doubt, access conflict, isolation uncertainty, public interface risk or unexpected ground conditions through the Estates contact.

Works Close-Out

Before leaving

Confirm barriers, drainage covers, temporary restrictions, photographs and any follow-up notes before the work area is released.

Scope Change

Scope change route opened. Capture the proposed change, affected area, plant movement, permit impact and Estates decision needed.

Access Issue

Access issue recorded. Check shared routes, visitor diversion, pedestrian exposure, neighbouring access and emergency access before movement continues.

Temporary restriction logged. Link the barrier, parking change, drainage cover, shared route limitation and expected removal time.

Isolation review requested. Confirm utility notes, buried-service uncertainty, authorised isolator and whether work must pause.

Inherited Risk

Inherited risk note opened. Record the local name, person, place, operational detail and verification needed before it is lost.

Drainage Access Change

Drainage access change recorded. Capture the revised boundary, cover condition, runoff concern and any temporary access control needed.

Environmental Concern

Environmental concern logged. Check silt movement, spill pathways, blocked drains, standing water and waterway protection before continuing.

Estates Contact Requested

Estates contact requested. Hold the activity where RAMS, access, isolation, public interface or inherited risk needs a named decision.

Works Complete

Works close-out started. Confirm covers, barriers, photos, temporary restrictions and follow-up actions before releasing the area.

Other contractor issue opened. Capture what was seen, where it is, who is affected and what decision is needed.
