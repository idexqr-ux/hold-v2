Goods In Workflow

Inbound delivery workflow for arrival, paperwork, pallet condition, Bay A12 validation and exceptions.

Inbound Active

Arrival Check

12 awaiting

Confirm delivery reference, supplier, booked slot and driver instruction before unloading at Bay A12.

Paperwork Review

Match delivery note, purchase order and pallet count before goods are accepted into the intake queue.

Pallet Condition

Visible check

Check wrapping, stability, labels and visible damage. Photograph exceptions before movement.

Bay A12 Validation

Use Bay A12 as the intake control point. Keep the delivery in queue order unless supervisor priority changes.

Movement Decision

Accepted pallets move to stock routing. Damaged, short or disputed pallets move to Hold Area B for review.

Human Judgement

Damage Reported

Damage report opened for the inbound delivery. Keep the pallet visible and attach photos before routing continues.

Mismatch Reported

Mismatch recorded between paperwork, delivery reference or pallet count. Supervisor review is required before acceptance.

Supervisor Requested

Supervisor request raised with delivery, Bay A12 and intake context attached.

Hold Area B Move

Hold Area B movement prepared for the inbound exception. Apply hold label before leaving the pallet.
