Accountability Trail

Operational understanding helps people decide what to do next. Accountability trails show how the current understanding was reached, reviewed and carried forward.

Supporting traceability

Governance and traceability

Accountability Trail sits behind operational understanding.

It is not the main user journey. It supports trust, governance and review by showing how information, changes, reviews and ownership were carried forward.

Information

Shows the records, observations and context that contributed to the current understanding.

Review

Shows where human review, confirmation or escalation shaped what is being carried forward.

Ownership

Shows who owns the current understanding and where responsibility sits across the operational environment.
