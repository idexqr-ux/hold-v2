Isolation Points

Verify location

Confirm the isolation cabinet, plant room access, water route and electrical ducting before any shutdown or disturbance.

Shutdown Sequence

Sequence controlled

Use the agreed shutdown sequence. Confirm affected tenants, dependent equipment, restart steps and whether temporary access controls are needed.

Authorised Isolation

Named person

Only authorised people may isolate or reinstate services. Record who instructed, who isolated and who confirmed re-energisation.

Shutdown Responsibility

Named owner

Before shutdown, confirm who owns the instruction, who communicates with affected users and who signs off reinstatement.

Buried-Service Uncertainty

Historic records

Partial electrical ducting records and the legacy water route need service scan confirmation before excavation or intrusive work.

Emergency Escalation

Escalate now

Use emergency escalation for suspected live service damage, uncontrolled leak, exposed conductor, fire risk or unsafe access to the cabinet.

Inherited Knowledge

Human memory

Historic records are incomplete. Janet knows where the valve really is; capture that memory and verify it against the current site condition.

Environmental Awareness

Water pathway

Water shutoff, leaks, runoff, drain exposure and spill pathways should be visible during isolation and reinstatement.

Other Isolation Issue

Human judgement

Use this where the isolation concern does not fit the plan but needs operational judgement before work continues.

Location Check

Location check requested. Confirm cabinet, valve, plant access, route labels, map reference and any local knowledge before touching services.

Shutdown Sequence

Shutdown responsibility check opened. Confirm instruction owner, affected users, communication route, reinstatement authority and completion sign-off.

Buried-Service Concern

Buried-service concern logged. Pause intrusive activity until records, scans, local knowledge and supervisor decision are aligned.

Emergency Escalation

Emergency escalation opened. Confirm danger type, exact location, access route, people affected and whether emergency services are required.

Inherited Knowledge

Inherited knowledge note opened. Capture who knows the true valve, what they know, where it applies and how it should be verified.

Environmental Risk

Environmental risk recorded. Check water pathway, drains, runoff, leak source, spill controls and downstream protection.

Other Isolation Issue

Other isolation issue opened. Capture the uncertainty, location, people affected and the decision needed before work continues.
