          Business Value Observation
          
        
          Organisations often hold information across multiple systems, formats and teams.
          
        
          The systems themselves are not usually the problem.
          
        
          The challenge occurs at the boundaries between them.
          
        
          Hold is evolving as a context layer that helps connect understanding across those boundaries.
          
        
          
          Existing systems manage information.
          Hold helps connect understanding.
          AI can then assist from better context.
