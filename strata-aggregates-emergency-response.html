Strata Aggregates — Emergency Response Orientation Map

Immediate Response

Critical Incident Actions

  1. Raise site alarm and notify emergency services if required
  2. Direct all personnel to the emergency assembly point
  3. Confirm plant shutdown or isolation where safe to do so
  4. Establish incident lead and control point
  5. Provide responder pack and orientation map to attending services

Responder Access

Emergency Access Routes

  • Primary Access North Gate via quarry access road
  • Secondary Access East Gate emergency access route
  • Emergency Rendezvous Main assembly area unless unsafe or redirected by incident lead
  • Restricted Hazard Zone No access unless authorised by site incident control

Hazard / Restricted Areas

Known Emergency Risks

  • Fuel & Environmental Compound Hazardous materials storage and spill risk
  • Crushing Plant Mechanical entrapment, stored energy and shutdown requirements
  • Restricted Hazard Zone No access during active restriction or unstable ground condition
  • Environmental Control Pond Spill containment resource, deep water and unstable edge risk
