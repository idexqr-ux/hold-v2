Hold V11

Understanding What Might Change the Answer

Most workplace conclusions are made with incomplete certainty.

The question is not only: What do we know?

It is also: What might make us understand this differently?

V11 explores the evidence conditions that might challenge, weaken, strengthen or alter the current understanding.
