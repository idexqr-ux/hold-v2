Authorised Works

Current scope

Work is limited to the marked-up plan, approved contractor method and named working area. Do not extend scope without Estates approval.

RAMS & Permit Controls

Before work

Confirm RAMS, insurance, permit status, hot works needs, isolation needs and supervisor release before unloading plant or materials.

Restricted Areas

Exclusion zones

Keep the eastern service corridor, turning area, soft verge and utility compound entrance clear unless explicitly authorised.

Buried Services

Do not disturb

Eastern service corridor records remain incomplete. Confirm scans, marked exclusions and isolation status before breaking ground or moving covers.

Inherited Site Risks

Verify locally

Historic utility routes and ground conditions may not match the plan. Stop and escalate unexpected covers, ducts, services or soft ground.

Environmental Awareness

Wet weather

Soft verge, standing water, silt movement and spill pathways should be visible before plant movement or ground disturbance.

Escalation Route

Named decision

Escalate permit doubt, access conflict, isolation uncertainty, buried-service concern or public/shared access issue before continuing.

Scope Change

Scope change opened. Capture the change, location, permit impact, additional plant or materials and approval needed.

Permit Review

Permit review requested. Check RAMS, insurance, isolation requirements, hot works controls, restricted areas and supervisor release.

Restriction Issue

Restriction issue recorded. Confirm exclusion zone, shared route, turning area, soft verge and temporary control requirements.

Buried Services

Buried-service concern opened. Pause ground disturbance and confirm scans, marked exclusions, isolation status, local knowledge and supervisor decision.

Inherited Risk

Inherited risk note opened. Record the local knowledge, who supplied it, where it applies and what verification is needed.

Environmental Concern

Environmental concern logged. Check runoff, silt, spill path, standing water and ground condition before authorising continued work.

Supervisor Requested

Supervisor requested. Hold activity where permit, access, isolation, public interface or inherited risk needs a named decision.

Other Contractor Issue

Other contractor issue opened. Capture the concern, location, affected people or routes and the decision needed.
