Before Work Starts

Contractor Requirements

  1. Review latest marked-up operational plan
  2. Confirm permit, RAMS and insurance status
  3. Check exclusion zones before unloading plant or materials
  4. Confirm low-loader access route if heavy equipment is arriving
  5. Report unexpected ground or service findings before continuing
