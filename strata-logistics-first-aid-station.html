First Aid Actions

Use these actions to record checks, report missing items and preserve the audit trail.

Confirm Kit Inspection Complete

Record that the first aid kit inspection has been completed and no immediate replenishment or expiry issue has been identified.

Report Missing / Used Item

Record the missing or used item, note whether the kit remains operational, and flag urgent replenishment if required.

Request Replenishment

Record the item required, current stock level and urgency before submitting the replenishment request.

Report Expired Item

Record the expired item, remove it from available use, and request a compliant replacement.

Open Incident Reporting Record

Open or create an incident reporting record where first aid use relates to an injury, near miss or reportable operational event.

Escalate H&S Concern

Escalate concerns where repeated kit use, missing stock or access issues suggest a wider health and safety risk.

Other First Aid Issue

Use this route where the issue does not fit an existing category but should still be retained in the operational audit trail.

Missing / Expired Stock Procedure

If required first aid stock is missing, used, damaged or expired, update Hold and request replenishment before the next scheduled inspection.

  • Used, missing or expired items reported through Hold automatically enter replenishment workflow and remain open until replacement stock is confirmed.
  • Mark urgent shortages where minimum stock level is affected.
  • Request replenishment from the H&S Coordinator or warehouse office.
  • Do not mark the kit complete until required stock is restored.

Workflow Notes

  • First aid stock issues appear in the operational review queue where action is required.
  • Inspection completion is retained for H&S audit purposes.
  • Used or expired items should remain open until replenishment is confirmed.
  • Incident reporting access should remain available to authorised staff.
