Fuel Storage Status

Active storage

Confirm tank condition, bund state, levels, waste-oil area and delivery status before fuel activity continues.

Authorised Access

Access should stay limited to trained operators, supervisors and authorised delivery or collection personnel.

Spill Prevention

Spill kit checked

Hoses, nozzles, caps, drip trays, spill kit and drain protection should be ready before handling fuel or oils.

Fuel Security

Locks, access record, delivery notes and unusual level changes should remain visible as security controls.

Environmental Risk

Bund water, surface residue, nearby drains, waste oil and additives can create wider environmental exposure.

Fire / Ignition Awareness

Keep clear

Ignition sources, hot works, smoking, damaged containers and poor separation require immediate attention.

Refuelling Controls

Refuelling should be supervised through authorised routes, with plant stable, engines controlled and spill response ready.

Emergency Response

Immediate escalation

Spill, fire, uncontrolled release or exposure risk should trigger site emergency routes and containment actions.

Documentation & Inspection

Due in 6 days

Inspection records, delivery notes, waste transfer paperwork and defect evidence should stay with the store record.
