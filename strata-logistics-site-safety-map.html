Important Instructions

  • Proceed to the nearest safe exit in an emergency.
  • Follow marked pedestrian routes where available.
  • Report immediately to the designated muster point after evacuation.
  • Do not cross active vehicle routes unless directed by the site responsible person or emergency services.
  • Drivers and visitors should follow site safety instructions before entering operational areas.
