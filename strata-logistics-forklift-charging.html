Forklift Charging Station

Safe charging, fault reporting and battery-handling guidance for the Strata Logistics charging bay.

EQ-07 / Active

Operational record

Charging procedure

Standard operating steps for safe forklift charging and equipment handover.

  1. Park forklift fully within the marked charging bay.
  2. Lower forks to ground level and engage the parking brake.
  3. Turn ignition off before disconnecting the battery.
  4. Inspect the charging cable for visible damage before use.
  5. Connect charger lead securely to the charging port.
  6. Confirm the charging indicator displays green.
  7. Record any fault immediately before leaving the bay.

Safety controls

Before charging

Battery handling

Safety gloves must be worn when handling battery connections. Do not charge forklifts showing signs of battery leakage.

Charging bay condition

The charging bay must remain clear of packaging, combustible materials and loose operational equipment.

Damaged equipment

Chargers with exposed wiring, damaged plugs or uncertain indicator status must not be used.

Fault reporting

Escalation steps

If battery damage, leakage or charger fault is identified, follow the steps below before the equipment is returned to use.

1. Stop use

Stop use immediately and isolate the equipment where safe to do so.

2. Mark unavailable

Attach an “Out of Service” tag to the affected charger or vehicle.

3. Report fault

Use the action below to log the maintenance fault for review.

4. Notify urgent concern

Notify Facilities on extension 4421 for urgent safety concerns.
