Forklift Charging Station

Charging bay visibility for authorised charging, cable hazards, battery safety, fire response and defects.

Charging Bay Active

Charging Bay Status

EQ-07 active

Charging Bay 2 is available. Keep the marked bay clear of packaging, pallets and loose equipment.

Authorised Charging

Only authorised operators should connect chargers, isolate equipment or handle battery connections.

Cable / Trip Hazards

Keep clear

Check charger lead, plug, cable route and floor condition before connecting or leaving the bay.

Battery Safety

Do not charge equipment with leakage, swelling, damaged connectors, exposed wiring or uncertain indicator status.

Fire Response

Escalate fast

Battery heat, smoke, burning smell or electrical fault requires immediate isolation if safe and urgent escalation.

Charging Actions

Audit Visibility

Charging status, defects, battery concerns, fire escalation and authorisation checks stay linked to the equipment record.

Human Judgement

If charger behaviour, battery condition or bay safety feels wrong, stop use and record the issue before leaving.

Authorised Charging

Authorised charging confirmation opened with charging bay, operator and equipment context attached.

Cable Hazard

Cable or trip hazard recorded. Keep the bay clear and prevent use if the charger lead is unsafe.

Battery Concern

Battery concern raised. Isolate if safe and prevent the affected forklift returning to use until reviewed.

Charger Defect

Charger defect report opened for exposed wiring, damaged plug, uncertain indicator status or bay equipment fault.

Fire Response

Fire response escalation opened. Raise alarm, clear the charging bay and call Facilities on extension 4421 if safe.

Other Charging Issue

Capture a charging concern that does not fit the standard battery, cable, defect or fire routes.
